📄 Summary: VPN Benefits and Limitations

✅ Benefits:

  • Hides real IP address → Enhances privacy
  • Encrypts internet traffic → Prevents ISP/attackers from spying
  • Allows bypassing geo-restrictions (e.g., content blocked in India)
  • Prevents tracking on public Wi-Fi

⚠️ Limitations:

  • Free plans may have speed or location limitations
  • VPN cannot protect from malware, phishing, or unsafe websites
  • Not completely anonymous — VPN provider can still see your traffic (if not no-log)
  • Slight drop in internet speed depending on server load and distance
